Bag Coach

A proof-of-concept: on-device pose tracking checks your guard, balance and punch type, and whether you actually landed the shot. Nothing is recorded or uploaded — the video never leaves your phone. Prop the phone side-on (~90°) to you at the bag, then tap Enable camera. A quick walkthrough opens the first time. (For the front-on slip drill, use Reaction back in the menu.)

Prop the phone side-on (~90°), tap your bag to mark it, then throw single shots.
