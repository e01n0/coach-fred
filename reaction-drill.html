Reaction Drill

A proof-of-concept slip drill: on-device pose tracking flashes SLIP LEFT / RIGHT at random and scores how fast you move the right way. Nothing is recorded or uploaded — the video never leaves your phone. Prop the phone front-on (facing you), then tap Enable camera. A quick walkthrough opens the first time.

Front-on drill — prop the phone facing you, stand centered, guard up, and tap Calibrate to set your neutral pose.
